No Frillo Brillo

The no frillo brillo cleaning option is our standard cleaning for customers who are looking to receive normal unit cleanings with no ameneties or consumer packaged goods offered. Costs for this option can vary from fifty to sixty dollars depending on size of the rooms you are booking to be cleaned . The no frillo brillo package can only be selected if you are able to book seven to ten cleanings a week for your units. Cleaning add ons such as cabinets ,dishes,and the fridge are charged seperately.
