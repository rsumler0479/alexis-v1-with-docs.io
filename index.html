Home Cleaning

Do I need to leave cleaning supplies for the maintenance engineers ?

No. We provide our maintenance engineers with the neccesary supplies to clean your space. If you want us to utilize your cleaning equipment please let us know when booking a cleaning.

Home Cleaning

What if I have a special cleaning request ?

All special cleaning requests can be included when you book a cleaning. If the special cleaning request is unfamiliar to our cleaning practices , we will contact you shortly after to inform you of any additional costs that may be added because of the request.

Home Cleaning

Do I need to be home when the maintenance engineers arrive ?

We leave this decision up to you. When you book a cleaning online , you will have to provide us with instructions on entry. Whether you choose to leave your key with someone you trust or wait for our maintenance engineers to arrive at the location , the decision is based upon what you're most comfortable with.

Home Cleaning

Do you clean up after recent home renovations ?

Absolutely ! To set this kind of cleaning up , message us here and we'll set up the appropraite accomadations for the task.

How does Earth Sky work?

We provide an online booking option that enables you to schedule a cleaning appointment with one of our maintenance engineers right from your computer. Once the Earth Sky maintenance engineers arrive and are let in they will begin cleaning. If you don't want to schedule an appointment online you can either email us at earthskycleaning@gmail.com or give us a call at (347)875-0647.

Home Cleaning

Does the cleaning take place in the morning or at night ?

You can schedule a cleaning for both day and night. Our service is in operation 24/7.
